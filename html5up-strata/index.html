Welcome to Café Harmony, a new and exciting virtual café chain! The café specializes in offering a variety of drinks, snacks, and light meals to customers. Over the past year, Café Harmony has seen impressive growth but now faces new challenges as the company is scaling to meet customer demand. The café has multiple locations across the city, each with its own performance metrics and unique customer preferences.

INSIGHTS

Sales show a generally positive trend with some seasonal dips.

Recommendation: Maintain current sales strategies, but introduce targeted promotions during slower months to boost revenue

Muffin, Cappuccino, and Iced Tea are the top-selling products.

Recommendation: Focus marketing and upselling on these high-performing items. Consider bundling or loyalty rewards. Review and improve low-performing menu

Adult and Elder groups drive the highest sales.

Recommendation: Tailor promotions and menu offerings to appeal to elder and Adult segments. Explore targeted campaigns for Youth and Aged to boost their engagement.

Milk, Croissant Dough, Iced Tea Bags, Tea bags and Muffin Mix frequently run low, while Products like Coffee Beans, Sandwich Bread were usually overstocked.

Recommendation: Automate inventory reordering for frequently low-stock items, while keeping overstocked items in check. Review supplier reliability and consider bulk purchasing and prioritizing the timing for ordering these Stock items.

Some employees consistently meet or exceed sales targets, others lag behind.

Recommendation: Recognize and reward top performers. Provide training and support for those below target. Share best practices across the team.

RECOMMENDATIONS FOR CAFE HARMONY

Overall Business Recommendation for Cafe Harmony Cafe harmony is performing well, with total revenue significantly exceeding expenses and strong sales from key menu items. However, there are clear opportunities to further strengthen the business:

• Leverage top-selling products: focus marketing and upselling efforts on muffin, cappuccino, and iced tea, which generate the most revenue. Consider bundling these items or introducing loyalty rewards to maximize their impact.

• Target high-value customer segments: the Adult and Elder demographics drive the highest sales. Tailor promotions, menu offerings, and customer experience to better serve these groups, while also developing strategies to engage youth and aged segments.

• Optimize inventory management: address frequent stockouts of key ingredients (e.g., Milk, croissant dough, iced tea bags, tea bags and muffin mix ) by automating reordering and reviewing supplier reliability. This will reduce lost sales and improve operational efficiency.

• Enhance employee performance: recognize and reward top-performing employees. Provide targeted training and support for those below sales targets, and encourage sharing of best practices to elevate overall team performance.

• Improve customer satisfaction: with an average rating just above 3 out of 5, there is room to enhance the customer experience. Analyze feedback to identify common issues, implement staff training, and adjust menu or service offerings as needed.

• Maintain financial discipline: continue monitoring expenses and revenue closely to sustain profitability. Use data-driven insights to guide investments in marketing, inventory, and staff development.
